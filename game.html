What is Cosmic Break?

Cosmic break is an action-packed online arena
shooting game featuring cute girls and killer robots.
Two teams of up to 10 players each are pitted
against each other in close-quarter arena battles.
A constantly changing battlefield will require you
to stay aware of your surroundings and adapt your strategies
accordingly.
